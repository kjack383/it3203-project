About the Internet

The Internet has transformed the way people communicate, learn, work, and share information. Beginning as a government research project, it has grown into one of the world's most important technologies.

This website explores the history, technologies, and key concepts that shaped the development of the Internet.

Key Terms

Term Description
ARPANET The first packet-switching network and the foundation of today's Internet.
TCP/IP The communication protocol used to transfer data across networks.
World Wide Web A system of websites and online resources accessed through browsers.
HTML The standard markup language used to create web pages.
